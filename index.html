About Us

Pulpleaf is established and registered on September 13, 2018, as an enterprise catering to all types of paper and office supplies requirements. The demand to supply paper products to various customer demographics including but not limited to hotels, offices, schools, hospitals, initially became the idea in establishing the company and growing to serve the increasing demand of these items within southern Metro Manila, Cavite, and Laguna.

Business Type:

Distributor of paper products, general office supplies, office equipment, school supplies, janitorial supplies, pantry items, and other general merchandise.

Product Range:

Paper products: Carbonless, Copy paper, Onion skin, Newsprint, Colored bond, etc.

Office and School supplies: Pens, envelopes, folders, ink, organizers, etc.

Mission

We are delighted to make our customers feel comfortable, open, and assured whenever we deliver our total package. We aim to achieve this by providing excellent quality of goods and service from our dedicated team who are always readily available to synergize with a smile.
